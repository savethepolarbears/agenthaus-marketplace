---
description: Analyze current social media trends for a given topic and suggest high‑impact content strategies. Usage: `/social-media:analyze-trend <topic>`
---
When invoked with "$ARGUMENTS" (the topic), perform a trend analysis:

1. **Research**: Search recent posts, news, and discussions about the topic across major platforms (Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook) to identify emerging patterns.
2. **Key hooks**: List three to five trending angles or hooks that are resonating with audiences (e.g., questions, controversial takes, new data).
3. **Format recommendations**: For each platform, recommend specific post formats (thread, carousel, short video) and content types (facts, stories, memes) that align with current trends.
4. **Call to action**: Suggest the next step, such as invoking the `content-writer` agent to generate posts based on the selected strategy.

Return a structured summary of your findings. Avoid hallucinating; rely on general knowledge and verified trends only.
