description: Compose a friendly Facebook post for a given topic with an optional tone.
---
Given "$ARGUMENTS" as input (topic[, tone]), create a Facebook post:

- **Length**: Aim for one to two medium‑length paragraphs totalling approximately 300–500 characters.
- **Tone**: Use a warm, conversational tone by default; adapt to the specified tone if provided.
- **Content**: Share a story, opinion, or useful information about the topic. Keep it authentic and relatable.
- **Engagement**: Encourage community interaction through a question or a call to action.
- **Hashtags**: Include one to three relevant hashtags if appropriate, but avoid over‑tagging.
