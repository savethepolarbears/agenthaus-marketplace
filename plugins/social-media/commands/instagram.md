description: Create an engaging Instagram caption for a topic with an optional tone.
---
Given "$ARGUMENTS" as input (topic[, tone]), write an Instagram caption:

- **Style**: Use a vivid, storytelling style that draws the reader in.
- **Tone**: Adapt the vibe according to the specified tone (for example, inspirational, humorous, or educational). If none is provided, default to a warm, expressive tone.
- **Emojis**: Suggest appropriate emojis throughout the caption to enhance emotion without overusing them.
- **Hashtags**: Include up to ten popular and relevant hashtags at the end of the caption.
- **Engagement**: Include a call to action encouraging followers to like, comment, save, or share the post.
